NoteTube Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [27/08/25]

Data Collection and Usage

NoteTube is designed with your privacy in mind. Here's how we handle your data:

  • Transcript Data: When you use NoteTube to extract YouTube transcripts, all processing happens locally in your browser. We do not collect or store your transcript data on our servers.
  • Screenshots: Any screenshots you capture are processed locally and never sent to our servers.
  • AI Features: If you choose to use AI features, your transcript text and selected screenshots are sent directly to Google's Generative Language API using your provided API key. We do not store or log this data.
  • API Keys: Your Google Gemini API key is stored locally in your browser's storage and is only used to make direct API calls to Google's services.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal data to third parties, except:

  • Data you explicitly choose to send to Google's API services when using AI features

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
